Animals with an estimated date of birth (on shelter)
###
Auditing
###
3300/Any
###
Displays animals on shelter with an estimated date of birth by date brought in
###
en
###
$VAR from DATE Enter from date$
$VAR to DATE Enter to date$

SELECT animal.animalname, animal.dateofbirth, animal. displaylocation AS location, species. speciesname, breed. breedname 
FROM animal
INNER JOIN breed
ON animal. breedid=breed. id 
INNER JOIN species
ON animal. speciesid=species. id
WHERE estimateddob=1 AND archived=0 AND DateBroughtIn >= '$@from$' AND DateBroughtIn <= '$@to$' 
ORDER BY speciesname, animalname
