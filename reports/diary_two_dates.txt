Shows all incomplete diary notes between two dates.
###
en
###
SELECT *
FROM diary
WHERE DateCompleted Is Null
AND DiaryDateTime >= '$ASK DATE Notes between$' AND DiaryDateTime <= '$ASK DATE and$'
ORDER BY DiaryForName, DiaryDateTime
